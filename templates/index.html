What Does This Tool Analyze?

The BDD Framework Analyzer evaluates your project's Behavior Driven Development implementation by examining:

  • Feature Files: Quality, organization, and best practices of your feature definitions
  • Step Definitions: Implementation quality, reusability, and maintainability
  • Test Coverage: How well your step definitions cover your feature scenarios
  • Framework Structure: Organization and architectural best practices
